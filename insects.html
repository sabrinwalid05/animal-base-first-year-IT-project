nsects are small animals that have six legs, and most of them have wings. They have three main body parts: the head, the thorax (middle), and the abdomen (end). Some insects you may know are ants, butterflies, bees, and ladybugs. Insects live almost everywhere — in gardens, trees, water, and even in your backyard! They help flowers grow by carrying pollen, and some help clean nature by eating dead plants or animals.
